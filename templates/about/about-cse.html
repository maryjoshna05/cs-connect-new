Department Overview

Excellence in computing education since 2002

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at AISAT is dedicated to fostering excellence in computing education, research, and innovation. Established in 2002, our department has consistently produced skilled engineers who excel in both industry and academia.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations, we provide a comprehensive learning environment that nurtures technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and ethical values. Our curriculum is designed to meet global standards while addressing local industry needs.

Vision & Mission

Guiding our department towards excellence

Vision

To develop a leading centre for creating highly competent computer science and engineering professionals who are technically sound, employable and socially committed towards sustainable development and growth.

Mission

  • Groom competent computer science engineers by providing high-quality professional training and a research-oriented ambience in alignment with sustainable development initiatives.
  • Enhance innovation, leadership qualities, team spirit, and ethical value-based living through multidisciplinary collaboration for sustainable societal progress.
  • Promote IT industry interaction and encourage entrepreneurship skills among students.

    • Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

    Graduates of Computer Science and Engineering shall

    1

    Evolve

    Evolve as globally competent IT professionals, researchers and entrepreneurs in the field of Computer Science.

    2

    Develop

    Develop innovative computing solutions for real world problems.

    3

    Demonstrate

    Demonstrate communication skills, leadership skills, ethical values and team work in IT professions.

    Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

    Graduates of Computer Science and Engincering will be able to

    PS1

    Design and develop computer programs and computer based systems of moderate complexity in the areas relevant to the current computational demands of the global society.

    PS2

    Gather knowledge in the various domains of computer science and identify a suitable career path to succeed in life.

    PS3

    Apply the fundamentals of computer science in order to evaluate potential risks and provide creative solutions to meet the needs of society.

    Department Milestones

    Key achievements marking our journey of excellence

    2002

    Department Establishment

    CSE Department established with first B.Tech batch of 60 students, marking the beginning of our journey in computing education.

    2008

    Publications

    Faculty and students started research publications in reputed international journals and conferences.

    2012

    Research Lab Setup

    Established advanced research laboratories for IoT, AI, and Network Security, fostering innovation and research culture.

    2015

    M.Tech Program Launch

    Introduced M.Tech program in Computer Science, expanding our academic offerings to postgraduate research education.

    2018

    100% Placement Achievement

    Achieved unprecedented 100% placement record with students placed in top MNCs like TCS, Infosys, and Wipro.

    2020

    Industry Collaboration

    Established MOUs with leading tech companies for internships, training programs, and collaborative research projects.

    2022

    AI Lab Inauguration

    Launched state-of-the-art AI and Machine Learning lab equipped with high-performance computing infrastructure.

    2024

    NBA Accreditation

    Received NBA accreditation, validating our commitment to quality education and continuous improvement in academic standards.
